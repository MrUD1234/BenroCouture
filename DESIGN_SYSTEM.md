# BenroCouture Design System

## Design Philosophy

Luxury lives in restraint.

The design should prioritize:

- Large visuals
- Strong typography
- Generous whitespace
- Minimal text
- Clean composition

The design should feel timeless rather than trendy.

---

## Typography

### Headings

Cormorant Garamond

Purpose:

- Hero headlines
- Section titles
- Editorial statements

Tone:

- Elegant
- Fashion-forward
- Premium

---

### Body Text

Inter

Purpose:

- Navigation
- Paragraphs
- Forms
- Buttons

Tone:

- Modern
- Clean
- Readable

## Visual Language

Use:

- Full-width photography
- Editorial layouts
- Cinematic imagery
- Large typography
- Spacious sections

Avoid:

- Glassmorphism
- Heavy gradients
- Excessive shadows
- Overly rounded cards
- SaaS UI patterns

---

## Border Radius

0px–12px maximum

Avoid pill-heavy interfaces.

---

## Shadows

Minimal.

Most depth should come from spacing and photography.

---

## Animations

Use:

- Fade In
- Fade Up
- Image Reveal
- Gentle Scale
- Slow Scroll Motion

Avoid:

- Bounce
- Flash
- Elastic effects
- Aggressive motion

---

## Spacing

Desktop section spacing:

120px–160px

Mobile spacing:

80px–100px

Generous whitespace is required.

---

## Photography Style

Images should feel:

- Warm
- Premium
- Editorial
- Sophisticated

Use consistent color grading.

Avoid overly saturated imagery.

---

## Hero Section

Use cinematic video.

Dark overlay.

Text must remain readable.

Video supports the story.

Video should never overpower the content.
