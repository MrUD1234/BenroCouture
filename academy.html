Why Learn At BenroCouture

Fashion Is Not Taught From A Book. It Is Learned By Doing.

Mrs. Ene believes that true fashion education happens in the studio, not in a lecture hall. Every student at BenroCouture learns by working with fabric, scissors, and sewing machines from the very first day.

Training follows international standards. Students master pattern drafting, garment construction, and professional finishing techniques used in global fashion houses. Beyond technical skills, they acquire business knowledge — pricing, client communication, and how to run a fashion enterprise.

Each student receives personal mentorship. Classes remain intentionally small so that every learner gets individual attention, direct feedback, and the guidance needed to develop real confidence.

What You Will Learn

Four Pillars Of Fashion Mastery

Pattern Drafting & Cutting

Learn to translate design ideas into precise patterns and cut fabric with professional accuracy.

Garment Construction

Master the art of assembling garments with clean seams, proper structure, and enduring quality.

Professional Embellishment

Develop the intricate skills of beading, embroidery, and ornamental finishing that define couture fashion.

Professional Finishing

Perfect the final details — pressing, hemming, and quality checks that separate exceptional work from ordinary.

Every class at BenroCouture is held in a working fashion studio. Students learn alongside experienced professionals, working with industry-grade equipment and real fabrics. The atmosphere is focused, creative, and supportive — a space where skill is built through practice, not theory.

Meet Your Mentor

Mrs. Ene

Founder & Lead Fashion Instructor

Mrs. Ene has spent years perfecting her craft and believes that fashion is a skill best passed from hand to hand. Her teaching philosophy is simple: teach what is practical, set high standards, and give every student the individual attention they deserve.

She built BenroCouture on the conviction that Nigerian fashion professionals can compete on any global stage when they are trained with the right techniques, the right discipline, and the right mindset. Every student who walks through her studio door is treated as a future professional.

"I don't just teach fashion. I teach my students how to think like designers, work like professionals, and believe in their own ability to create."

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

Do I need previous experience?

No. The program is designed for beginners and those with some experience alike. Training is tailored to your current level.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every student who completes the program receives a certificate from BenroCouture Fashion Academy.

What will I learn?

Pattern drafting, garment construction, professional embellishment, finishing techniques, pricing, client management, and fashion entrepreneurship.

Is training practical?

Entirely. You will work with fabric and machines from the first class. There are no theoretical-only sessions.

Where is the academy located?

At BenroCouture Studio, No. 34 Alaenyi Street, Owerri, Imo State, Nigeria.

How long does training last?

The program duration is flexible. Contact us during your consultation for specific details based on your schedule and goals.
